About Us

The Seattle RedBar chapter is a group of watch collectors and enthusiasts that meets regularly in the Greater Seattle Area.

The RedBar Crew has quickly grown from being a NYC meet-up for watch enthusiasts into a global organization with multiple chapters that provide a safe space for aficionados to meet other aficionados.

About RedBar

The RedBar Group's goals include fostering the growth of existing collector communities around the globe, helping fellow enthusiasts start new meetups in their local areas, and encouraging collectors to give back to the local communities where our gatherings take place.

The #redbarcrew hashtag on Instagram is avidly followed by horology enthusiasts around the globe.
